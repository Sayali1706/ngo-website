All About Us

Veer Savarkar Samajik Evam Shaikshanik Sansthan is a non-profit charity organisation inspired by lifelong social work of the great indian freedom fighter Swatantryaveer Vinayak Damodar Savarkar.He was not just a freedom fighter but he was a bold warrior, good orator, prolific writer, a poet, a historian, a philosopher,a social worker.VSSESS is based in Bihar,established in 2008 and working in all India to provide education to society and to encourage the weaker section through various empowerment programs.

Our AIM

Our organization aims to redeﬁne what is possible for unemployed educated youths,men & women from low-income communities to achieve through its network of various educational and skill development programs. It thus aims to bring reforms of strength, sustenance and dignity to the lives of weaker section of society.

VISION

To build up VSSESS through out the country as a preferred centre of learning for the poorest of the poor sections of the society with a focus on formal education & skill development aimed at providing sustainable livelihood and scope for all-round development through employment.

MISSION

Our various missions include awareness of sanitisation, programs for development of children labour,women labour & other labourers, run health care centres for public health program, rescue operation for affected people in Flood,famine, epidemic,draught and many more.
